# macro-research

> Quantitative tools and research frameworks for global macro analysis

---

### Overview

This repository is a personal research library of quantitative tools built to support rigorous analysis across global macro asset classes. Each project translates fundamental macro intuition into reproducible, data-driven frameworks — covering rates, foreign exchange, commodities, equities, and the geopolitical dynamics that move them.

The work here is grounded in the belief that disciplined quantitative methods and deep macro understanding are complementary, not competing. Models are built to inform judgment, not replace it.

---

### Research Philosophy

- **Macro first.** Every tool begins with an economic thesis. Data and code serve the research, not the other way around.
- **Fundamentals as the anchor.** Quantitative frameworks are grounded in fundamental analysis — monetary policy, growth dynamics, inflation, capital flows, and geopolitical risk. 
- **Reproducibility.** All analysis is documented, version-controlled, and designed to be auditable from raw data to final output.
- **Intellectual honesty.** Models have limits. Where assumptions are made, they are stated explicitly.
- **Continuous refinement.** This is a living body of work. Frameworks are updated as markets evolve and understanding deepens.

---

### Coverage

| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Rates & Fixed Income** | Yield curves, monetary policy, duration, credit spreads |
| **Foreign Exchange** | Rate differentials, carry, purchasing power, capital flows |
| **Commodities** | Energy, metals, agricultural — supply/demand and macro drivers |
| **Equities** | Macro regime analysis, sector rotation, earnings and valuation |
| **Macro Regimes** | Growth/inflation cycle classification, cross-asset implications |
| **Geopolitics & Policy** | Sanctions, trade policy, central bank dynamics, political risk |
